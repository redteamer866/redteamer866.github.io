Closing Shift Instructions

General Steps

  1. Count and log the cash in the register.
  2. Ensure all products are restocked and displayed neatly.
  3. Clean and sanitize the countertops and high-touch surfaces.
  4. Turn off all unnecessary lights and appliances.
  5. Secure the doors and set the alarm system.
  6. Leave the keys in the designated safe spot for the next shift.

Final Checks

  • Verify the windows are locked.
  • Empty all trash bins and take the trash to the dumpster.
  • Run a quick floor check for any forgotten items or hazards.
  • Confirm the alarm system is active before leaving.
