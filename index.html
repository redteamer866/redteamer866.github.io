Top Picks for Bottom-Shelf Wine

Two Buck Chuck (Charles Shaw)

Price: $2.99

Pair with: Chicken Ramen

A Trader Joe's classic that punches way above its price. Its light flavor complements the salty broth.

Barefoot Moscato

Price: $5.99

Pair with: Spicy Ramen

Sweet and bubbly, this wine tones down the heat in spicy ramen. Dessert in a glass!

Yellow Tail Shiraz

Price: $6.99

Pair with: Beef Ramen

A bold red that holds its own with hearty beef flavors. It's like steak and potatoes on a dime!

Sutter Home White Zinfandel

Price: $4.99

Pair with: Shrimp Ramen

Light, fruity, and perfect for seafood-inspired ramen dishes. A tropical escape on a budget.
